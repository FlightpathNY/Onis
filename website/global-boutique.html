What We Do

A Global Boutique
Dedicated to Creating Brand Value

Onis creates innovative apparel licensing opportunities for global brands. Through a highly nimble and agile logistics outsourcing model, we are able to by-pass the overhead issues our competitors face.

Supporting established global fashion brands, Onis creates best in class lifestyle apparel opportunities. We’ve brought together industry leaders in design, sourcing, sales, and marketing enabling us to capitalize on market changes and elevated consumer expectations.

The Business of Radical Innovation

Onis was founded in 2013 with the core belief that there was a far better way for quality global brands to realize significant value in apparel licensing.

We have developed innovative processes and capabilities to drive quality back into the mix by embodying our brand partner’s DNA through better fabrics, design and bringing game changing speed to market - 50% time reduction - to align brands with RTW trends.

Additionally, we bring worldwide sales ability throughout North America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East. Funded by long-term, private investors the Onis is positioned for significant and sustainable growth by being focused on strategic and global opportunities for our brand partners

Understanding Exceptional Brands

Onis speaks the language – design, material, sourcing, and merchandising - of exceptional brands and how to translate this value to apparel and to retail. We are fundamentally different from our competitors because we are brand curators and operators…not traditional “cost driven” producer/manufacturers.
