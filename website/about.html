Meet Our Team

Passion for doing it differently, and for finding a better way, is at the center of each of us. We are all believers in the idea that it may not be broken but we can fix it anyway.

JON LEWIS, CEO

JON LEWIS, CEO is a dynamic, results oriented senior executive with solid experience in both entrepreneurial and Fortune 500 companies. He has a record of achieving sales and profit goals in ever-increasing roles of responsibility. Jon has extensive experience in the intimate apparel category, along with women’s accessories and swimwear.

He has been at the C-level and a senior sales executive for almost 20 years. As a corporate manager Jon has built a reputation on his ability to assemble a high performance team, his leadership, and success in the marketplace.

ANDREW LANE, VP OF DESIGN

ANDREW LANE, VP OF DESIGN is one of the most talented designers within the Lingerie/ Swimwear category.

In his 16 year career, Andrew has worked in NYC with Narciso Rodriguez, Vera Wang, Donna Karan New York, and Tommy Hilfiger as well as La Perla and Triumph while designing in Europe for three years.

JOHN ELMUCCIO, VP OF SOURCING & OPERATIONS

JOHN ELMUCCIO, VP OF SOURCING & OPERATIONS has comprehensive experience working in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments. He has worked as a senior sales and operations executive with fortune 500 apparel companies.

John founded and sold an apparel company, managed acquisitions and serves as an industry consultant to private investment firms on global market trends in textiles and apparel. His executive background includes senior roles with Warnaco, Maidenform and Leslie Fay.

GIHAN GABOR, VP OF SALES

GIHAN GABOR, VP OF SALES has over 20 years’ experience ranging from buying, planning, and strategic selling. Gihan has proven herself an invaluable asset to some of the largest apparel companies in the world, working with brands such as Felina, Ralph Lauren, and Olga lingerie.

Having proven success with companies like Warnaco, Sara Lee, and Duty Free Stores, Gihan has demonstrated superior analytical capabilities coupled with strong communication skills that have made her a respected leader in the category.

SARAH SAUNDERS, DIRECTOR OF MARKETING

Having received a Masters of Art in painting from Pratt Institute, Sarah Saunders has cultivated an eye for beauty and design. Sarah has spent the last 13 years working as a head designer at floral event companies on the East Coast.

Sarah has worked closely with business owners to understand, execute and stay true to the company’s brand and voice.

SCOTT HERCKIS, CFO

SCOTT HERCKIS, CFO began the first 5 years of his career handling the audit and assurance department of Arthur Andersen where he worked on large corporate clients such as Sheraton hotels and Cadbury Schweppes. It was during this time that he earned his CPA.

Scott spent the next 10 years working his way up the accounting and finance ladder first as a controller, then as vice president of finance, and eventually as a CFO in the fashion and apparel industry.

GREG KANE, BRAND LIASON

GREG KANE, BRAND LIASON facilitates relations between clients and ōNIS.His work includes technical and support roles aiding management and the entire team.

Greg has worked in editorial and sales positions, while seeking his Bachelors Degree in a part-time capacity.

LESLIE URIAS, ASSISTANT PRODUCTION MANAGER

LESLIE URIAS, ASSISTANT PRODUCTION MANAGER has seven years of experience in the fashion industry, working in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments. She has been overseeing the production flow for multiple brands with factories overseas.

Her previous experience includes working with Jones Apparel Group, Judith Ripka and others. Leslie's previous experience in PR included working with high-end designers like Rag & Bone, Oliver Spencer, Sass & Bide and Thread Social. These experiences have enriched her high standards for production quality.

LUCY SHEKHTMAN, DIRECTOR OF DESIGN

LUCY SHEKHTMAN, DIRECTOR OF DESIGN has come to use with 10 + years in the industry specializing in swimwear. She has designed swim for such well-known brands as Michael Kors, Marc Jacobs, and Tommy Hilfiger, while also launching swim for Proenza Schouler and Kate Spade.
